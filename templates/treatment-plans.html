Diabetes Management Template

Template

Standard Protocol

Medication: Start Metformin 500mg BID, titrate to 1000mg BID over 4 weeks. Consider GLP-1 agonist or SGLT2 inhibitor based on comorbidities.

Goals: HbA1c <7.0%, weight loss 5-10%, BP <140/90 mmHg, LDL <100 mg/dL.

Diet: Medical nutrition therapy with carbohydrate counting. Emphasize whole grains, vegetables, lean protein.

Exercise: 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity per week. Strength training 2-3 days per week.

Monitoring: HbA1c every 3 months. Home glucose monitoring as indicated. Annual eye and foot exams.

Education: Diabetes self-management education (DSME) program. Carbohydrate counting instruction.

Hypertension Management Template

Template

Standard Protocol

Medication: First-line ACE inhibitor or ARB. Add thiazide diuretic or calcium channel blocker if needed.

Goals: BP <130/80 mmHg for diabetes patients, <140/90 mmHg for others. Assess for target organ damage.

Lifestyle: DASH diet with sodium restriction (<2,300 mg/day). Weight loss if overweight. Regular exercise.

Monitoring: Office BP measurements at each visit. Home BP monitoring for medication titration.

Assessment: Evaluate for secondary causes of hypertension. Screen for cardiovascular risk factors.
